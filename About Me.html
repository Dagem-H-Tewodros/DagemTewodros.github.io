About Me

Hi there! I’m Dagem Tewodros, a dedicated Data and Marketing Analyst based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. My passion lies in using data to tell stories that drive business decisions and uncover new opportunities. With a strong foundation in Economics and Logistics & Supply Chain Management, I’ve spent the last few years honing my skills in data analysis, visualization, and strategic marketing.

What I Do

I thrive on turning complex data into clear, actionable insights. Whether it’s digging into sales trends, predicting market behaviors, or building interactive dashboards, I love bringing clarity to the numbers. I’m well-versed in tools like SQL, Python, Tableau, Excel, and Power BI, which allow me to dive deep into data and surface insights that matter.

My Professional Experience

• Data Analyst, YAFOM Real Estate Marketing (March 2022 - October 2023): During my time at YAFOM, I led the charge in cleaning and standardizing data for over 2,100 property listings. I also developed a predictive pricing model using Scikit-learn, which not only improved pricing efficiency by 30% but also boosted inquiries by 20%. Creating dashboards in Excel and Power BI, I helped the team make faster, data-driven decisions.

• Marketing Analyst, Blu Courage Trading (December 2023 - August 2024): At Blu Courage, I analyzed more than 15,000 transactions to help increase our product market share. My work also involved developing Power BI dashboards that improved inventory control and ordering decisions, leading to a 22% year-over-year increase in import sales.

My Approach

I’m a firm believer that data is more than just numbers, it’s a powerful tool for making informed decisions and driving change. I approach every project with curiosity and a keen eye for detail, always aiming to bring fresh insights to the table. I’m also a lifelong learner, constantly exploring new trends and techniques to stay ahead in the ever-evolving world of data.

Background

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Economics from Unity University and am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Logistics and Supply Chain Management at AAU School of Commerce, set to graduate in July 2025. My academic background gives me a unique perspective, blending quantitative analysis with strategic business thinking.
